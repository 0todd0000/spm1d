.. _label-fctSPM:


(Matlab) Factorial SPM with *post hoc* tests
--------------------------------------------

Compute ANOVA and *post hoc* tests from anova1 to anova3rm, with a non-parametric approach (permutation tests).

`Repostiory @ GitHub <https://github.com/tramarobin/fctSPM>`_

`Code @ Matlab Central <https://www.mathworks.com/matlabcentral/fileexchange/77945-fctspm>`_
