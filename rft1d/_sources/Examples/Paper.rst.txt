Results from the paper
========================================================================================

These scripts are located in **./rft1d/examples/paper** and reproduce the figures from:

	Pataky TC (2015) RFT1D: Smooth One-Dimensional Random Field Upcrossing Probabilities in Python. **Journal of Statistical Software** (accepted for publication 18 April 2015).

.. note:: The dataset associated with Figures 13, 15, 16 and 17 is available in :ref:`rft1d.data.weather <label-API-data>`


Script list:

	- :red:`all_results.py` --- Single script which replicates all paper results.
	
	1. :red:`fig01_fields.py` --- 1D Gaussian fields
	#. :red:`fig02_EC_HC.py` --- Description of the Euler and Hadwiger characteristics
	#. :red:`fig03_EC.py` --- Euler characteristic validation
	#. :red:`fig04_fields1d_broken.py` --- pieceswise continuous 1D Gaussian fields
	#. :red:`fig05_EC_broken.py` --- topological validation for broken fields
	
	#. :red:`fig06_sf.py` --- survival function for 1D Gaussian fields
	#. :red:`fig07_bonf_sf.py` --- RFT vs. Bonferroni survival functions for rough fields
	#. :red:`fig08_bonf_isf.py` --- RFT and Bonferroni inverse survival functions crossing points
	#. :red:`fig09_val_maxima.py` --- validation for 1D Gaussian field maxima
	#. :red:`fig10_upcrossing.py` --- anatomy of field upcrossings
	
	#. :red:`fig11_val_cluster.py` --- validation for 1D Gaussian cluster-level inference
	#. :red:`fig12_val_set.py` --- validation for 1D Gaussian set-level inference
	#. :red:`fig13_weather_0_data.py` --- dataset overview
	#. :red:`fig14_fwhm_estimation.py` --- validation of field smoothness estimation
	#. :red:`fig15_weather_1_rft.py` --- parametric inference using RFT

	#. :red:`fig16_weather_2_wrapped.py` --- parametric inference, assuming a circular field
	#. :red:`fig17_weather_3_nonparam.py` --- nonparametric inference using an RFT-like procedure
