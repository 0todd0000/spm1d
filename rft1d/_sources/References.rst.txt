.. _label-SPMsoftware:

Software
---------------------------------

All packages below implement RFT-based inference,
and while they target primarily 3D field analysis,
can also be applied to 1D/2D field analysis. 

* `SPM8 <http://www.fil.ion.ucl.ac.uk/spm/>`_
* `fmriStat <https://github.com/matthew-brett/fmristat/>`_
* `NiPy <http://neuroimaging.scipy.org>`_
